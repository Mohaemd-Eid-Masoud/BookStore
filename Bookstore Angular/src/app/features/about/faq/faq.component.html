Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our bookstore

How do I place an order?

+

To place an order, simply browse our catalog, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to create an account or log in, then provide shipping and payment information to complete your purchase.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and other secure payment methods. All transactions are processed securely through encrypted connections.

How long does shipping take?

+

Standard shipping typically takes 3-7 business days within the continental US. Express shipping (1-2 business days) is available for an additional fee. International shipping times vary by destination.

Do you offer free shipping?

+

Yes! We offer free standard shipping on orders over $50 within the continental United States. Some restrictions may apply to oversized or international orders.

What is your return policy?

+

You can return most items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Items must be in their original condition and packaging. Return shipping costs may apply unless the return is due to our error.

How do I initiate a return?

+

To start a return, log into your account, go to your order history, and select the return option. You can also contact our customer service team for assistance with the return process.

How do I create an account?

+

Click the "Sign Up" button in the top navigation, fill out the registration form with your email address and a secure password, and verify your email address to complete the account creation process.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

+

Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page, enter your email address, and we'll send you a secure link to reset your password. The link will expire after 24 hours for security reasons.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

+

You can modify or cancel your order within 1 hour of placement if it hasn't entered the shipping process. Contact our customer service team immediately for assistance with order changes.

How do I track my order?

+

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order by logging into your account and viewing your order history for the latest updates.

Do you ship internationally?

+

Yes, we ship to many international destinations. Shipping rates and delivery times vary by country. Import duties and taxes may apply and are the responsibility of the recipient.

How can I contact customer support?

+

You can reach our customer support team through our contact page, by email at support@bookstore.com, or by phone at 1-800-BOOKS-4U (1-800-266-5748) during business hours.

Still need help?

If you couldn't find the answer to your question, please don't hesitate to contact our support team.
